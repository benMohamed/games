FAQs:

How do I play Flow 2048?

Connect matching numbers to merge them. When two tiles with the same number connect, they form a new tile with double the value. Keep merging to reach the 2048 tile!

Can I play without an internet connection?

Yes, Flow 2048 can be played offline.

How can I reset my progress?

You can reset your game progress by going to the settings menu and selecting "Reset Game."
